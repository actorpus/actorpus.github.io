Message from the Headteacher

Welcome to the Fringford Church of England Primary School website.

We are a small primary school with big ambitions for our children. Fringford Primary is a school where children feel safe within a happy, stimulating and nurturing environment in which each child is encouraged to enjoy learning and develop a lively and enquiring mind. Our school is a place where everyone feels welcome and valued. All children are encouraged to grow into confident and independent learners who strive to reach their true potential in all aspects of school life. Our pupils develop strong friendships, have fun and are respectful of each other and the environment.

I feel privileged to lead a team of professional, talented and committed staff who work together to provide our children with the very best learning opportunities.  The best interest of the children lies at the heart of everything we do. The positive support of our parents and the wider community is also very important to us and we have an open and welcoming environment where we can work in partnership for the benefit of our pupils.

This website offers an insight into our school. It provides us with a wonderful opportunity to share and celebrate the life and work of our pupils. It is also a vital tool in communicating with parents, carers and visitors as well as providing information about the various policies, practice and procedures in our school.

If you would like to know more please contact the school office and arrange a visit to see us at work.

I look forward to celebrating our successes with you on this site over the coming months and years.

Mr F Pastore

Headteacher Fringford C of E School
